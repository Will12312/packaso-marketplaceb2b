    Finding environmentally friendly and sustainable packaging is now easier!

    Packaso is working to streamline the sourcing process for packaging and promote the adoption of more sustainable options within the industry.

    The packaging sector is a major global industry, but it is still largely reliant on traditional methods such as in-person meetings, intermediaries, and personal referrals to obtain cosmetic packaging.

    However, the needs of the packaging industry are evolving as the speed of product development increases, e-commerce expands, and the state of the environment becomes a critical concern.

    In this context, there is a greater need than ever to facilitate the connection between supply and demand in packaging. To address this, Packaso is using technology to modernize the way that brands and packaging providers find and work with each other, consolidating these activities in one place.
